## Risk Assessment

Answer a few multiple-choice questions to assess how susceptible your course is to GenAI gaming. Receive clear, actionable feedback on changes you can make to improve academic integrity in the age of generative AI.

### Features
- Quick assessment through targeted questions
- Personalized risk evaluation for your course
- Actionable recommendations for improvement
- Evidence-based guidance on assessment design

### Use Cases
- Course design and redesign
- Academic integrity planning
- Assessment strategy development
- Understanding GenAI risks in education
