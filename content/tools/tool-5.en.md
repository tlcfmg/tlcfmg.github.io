## Detection Deluder

Detection Deluder helps you assess your ability to distinguish between real academic abstracts from your chosen discipline and their AI-generated counterparts. This tool demonstrates the challenges of detecting AI-generated content.

### Features
- View real abstracts alongside AI-generated versions
- Choose abstracts from various academic disciplines
- Track your detection accuracy
- Learn about the limitations of AI detection

### Use Cases
- Training educators on AI content detection challenges
- Demonstrating AI capabilities to students
- Building awareness about AI-generated content
- Research on human vs. AI text discrimination
