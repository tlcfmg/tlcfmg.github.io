## TLCBot

TLCBot is a chatbot that provides answers with clear sources based on content from the UvA Teaching and Learning Centre website. It helps educators and students find relevant information about teaching practices, tools, and resources.

### Features
- Answers questions based on TLC-FMG website content
- Provides clear source citations for all responses
- Context-aware conversations
- Accessible information about teaching resources

### Use Cases
- Quick access to TLC resources and information
- Support for educators seeking teaching advice
- Navigate TLC services and offerings
- Learn about available courses and workshops
